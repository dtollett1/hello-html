Saltwater Reef Tanks!

Background

As a family we have started to tinker with a small freshwater tank with a few snails and an angelfish. this new venture has sparked my curiosity, and drawn me to the complexity and beauty of saltwater reef tanks. While shopping for supplies for our freshwater tank I was instantly drawn to the elaborate and beautiful saltwater tanks with coral, Anemone and various other marine life.

Growing up, my grandmother owned a small hotel on the Gulf of Mexico in SWFL. Many summer days were spent swimming, snorkeling and interacting with the marine life right outside her back door.

Watching the reef tanks reminded me of my childhood and has inspired me to begin the endless amount of research (and money) it takes to build and sustain one of these incredible tanks.
